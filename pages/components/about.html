This web application runs entirely in your browser. All data is processed locally in your browser and is not transmitted anywhere. This benefit comes at the cost that the initial loading might take a while since we need to download all the resources (that would otherwise live on a server).

Contact

If you have any questions, suggestions, or feedback, please reach out to us: inchi@ac.rwth-aachen.de. We especially encourage you to report problematic structures (please include a molfile along with a description of the problem).
